# Major 72 Studio

**Engineering the Future of Professional-AI Hybrid Music Production**

Major 72 Studio (working title: Signal Foundry) is a professional-grade music production platform that combines generative AI with human creative judgment. Unlike generic AI music generators, Major 72 encodes the expertise of experienced producers into selectable "Creative Modes" — allowing anyone from independent artists to hobbyists to produce release-ready music guided by professional aesthetic standards.

## What It Does

- **Upload a rough idea** (bass line, vocal demo, guitar riff) and get a fully arranged, mixed, and mastered track
- **Choose a Creative Mode** that defines the sonic identity — from "Eddie Signature" (warm, organic-digital balance) to "Vintage Analogue" (1970s tape warmth)
- **Go live** — export professional sheet music, click tracks, and rehearsal packages to take AI productions to the stage
- **Hire session musicians** — replace AI stems with live recordings from world-class players via integrated marketplace
- **Personalize** — create custom songs for weddings, anniversaries, and milestones with zero technical knowledge

## The Vision

In a world of infinite synthetic content, professional creative judgment is the most valuable currency. Major 72 Studio encodes that judgment into software — bridging AI automation, real-world performance, and transparent ownership through blockchain-backed royalty infrastructure.
