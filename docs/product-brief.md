# Product Brief: Major 72 Studio

## Executive Summary

The AI music market is exploding — $2.4 billion in 2024, projected to hit $22 billion by 2035. Over 100 million people have used Suno to generate music from text prompts. But there's a problem: **80% of professional producers are neutral-to-negative about AI music tools**, and more than a third worry about "musical sameness." The AI music revolution has arrived, but professionals don't trust it.

Major 72 Studio solves this by encoding the creative judgment of experienced producers — starting with 20-year veteran Eddie Wilson — into selectable "Creative Modes" that guide AI output. Instead of replacing the human, the AI handles the technical drudgery (stem separation, mixing, mastering) while the producer's ear shapes the result. The output isn't generic AI — it's your music, polished through a professional's taste.

The platform launches as a four-tier service — **Hear It, Build It, Play It, Gift It** — starting with a $29-49/month MVP that takes a rough phone recording and returns a professionally mixed track in under 60 seconds. No competitor occupies this positioning: professional quality plus human-guided AI. The quadrant is empty, and the market is asking for exactly what we're building.

## The Problem

**For producers and independent artists:**
Every day, 50,000 AI-generated tracks flood streaming platforms. Spotify removed 75 million "spammy tracks" last year. The market is drowning in AI slop — and legitimate AI-assisted creators are caught in the crossfire. Meanwhile, the tools that exist force a binary choice: either you use a generic AI generator (Suno, Udio) and get output that sounds like everyone else's, or you spend $500-$2,000 on studio time you can't afford. There is no professional-grade AI tool that protects your creative identity while saving you time.

**For consumers:**
You want to give someone a custom song for their wedding. Songfinch charges $199-$399 and takes 7-14 days. The quality depends on which songwriter you get. There's a $75M/year market for this — but it's slow, expensive, and inconsistent.

**The gap:** Professional creative judgment — knowing when to push the low end, when to widen the stereo field, how to make a mix breathe — is the one thing AI can't generate on its own. It's also the one thing nobody has figured out how to scale. Until now.

## The Solution

Major 72 Studio is a **professional music production service** — not another AI generator — built on a four-tier model:

| Tier | Service | Price | Phase |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Hear It** | Upload rough idea → get it back polished in Eddie's signature sound | $29-49/mo | MVP |
| **Build It** | Full AI arrangement with Session Players | $79-149/track | Phase 2 |
| **Play It** | Stage-ready charts, click tracks, session musician hiring | $299-999/project | Phase 3 |
| **Gift It** | Custom song from your story (weddings, milestones) | $49-149/song | Soft-launch with MVP |

**The "Hear It" MVP in five steps:**
1. Upload any rough recording (phone, interface, DAW export)
2. Select "Eddie Signature" mode (one button)
3. Wait 30-60 seconds while the engine separates, mixes, and masters
4. Hear the before/after reveal — your music, transformed
5. Download polished mix + stems + session metadata

The engine doesn't add instruments or change your composition. It takes what you created and makes it sound like a professional spent six hours on it. That's the trust builder.

## What Makes This Different

**1. The empty quadrant.** Every competitor is either automated + consumer (Suno, Udio) or professional + manual (SoundBetter, Songfinch). No one is professional + AI-guided. We're the first.

**2. Named producer taste, not generic algorithms.** "Eddie Signature Mode" isn't a preset — it's a continuously calibrated logic profile built from a real producer's creative decisions. As the system captures Eddie's approve/reject feedback, it gets smarter. This scales human expertise without cloning it.

**3. Idea to stage.** No other platform connects AI production → score generation → live charts → session musician hiring. The AI-to-live performance pipeline doesn't exist anywhere else. We're building the infrastructure for it.

**4. Copyright-defensible by design.** Because users upload original material and make creative selections, the output is a copyrightable hybrid work under USCO guidance — unlike prompt-only generators whose output may be public domain.

**5. The AI slop antidote.** In a market flooded with generic AI tracks, "Verified Professional Production" backed by a named producer becomes a quality signal. We're not adding to the noise — we're the filter.

## Who This Serves

**Primary: The Cautious Experimenter** (~50% of all music producers)
Independent producers and artists, ages 25-45, running home or project studios. They've tried AI tools but haven't committed. They want time savings on technical work but are terrified of losing their sound. They're spending $15-40/month on fragmented tools (stem separation, mastering, samples). They'll switch to Major 72 when they hear their own music come back sounding professional. WTP: $30-50/month.

**Secondary: The Gift Buyer**
Non-musicians, ages 25-55, searching "custom song for wedding." They don't care how the music is made — they care that it sounds real and arrives fast. They're currently choosing between Songfinch ($199+, 2 weeks) and settling for a Spotify playlist. WTP: $49-149 per song.

## Scope

**In for MVP (Hear It + Gift It):**
- Audio upload + ingestion (WAV, MP3, FLAC, M4A, AIFF)
- Stem separation engine (12+ stems, <60 seconds)
- Eddie Signature Creative Mode (one mode at launch)
- Automated mixing and mastering pipeline
- Before/after comparison UI
- Polished mix + stem export
- Consumer custom song flow (separate brand/URL)
- Basic subscription billing

**Explicitly out for MVP:**
- Session Players / AI arrangement (Phase 2)
- Score generation / live charts (Phase 3)
- SoundBetter integration (Phase 3)
- Additional Creative Modes (Phase 2)
- Blockchain royalty infrastructure (Phase 4+)
- Producer Mode Marketplace (Phase 4+)
- DAW plugin integration (future)

## Vision

If "Hear It" works, the flywheel spins:

**Year 1:** Prove that AI + professional judgment produces results that cautious producers trust. Build the subscriber base. Validate Gift It as a viral consumer channel.

**Year 2:** Launch "Build It" — full arrangement — because Hear It users are asking for it. Launch "Play It" — live charts and session musicians — because Build It users want to take their tracks to the stage. Open Creative Modes to additional producers.

**Year 3:** Open the Producer Mode Marketplace — a new category where professional producers sell their unique taste profiles, like the sample pack and VST plugin markets before it. Major 72 becomes the infrastructure layer for professional AI-guided music production worldwide.

The endgame: in a world of infinite synthetic content, **the producer's ear becomes the most valuable asset in music**. Major 72 is how it scales.
